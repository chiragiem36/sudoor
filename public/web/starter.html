Advancia Electric | Starter

Overview

  • Direct On-Line(DOL) method is the simplest starting method for 3-phase pump set.
  • DOL starters are basic electrical starters with contractors and relay for ON/OFF operation and overload protection of the pump set.

TECHNICAL SPECIFICATIONS

  • Max motor output at 415V, 3-Ph - Upto HP: 7.5
    kW: 5.5
  • Relay Max. Range (A) - 21
  • Type - MK1 DOL

Advantages of DOL Starter

  • Most Economical and Cheapest Starter
  • Simple to establish, operate and maintain
  • Simple Control Circuitry
  • Easy to understand and trouble-shoot
  • It provides 100% torque at the time of starting
  • Only one set of cubie is required from starter to motor
